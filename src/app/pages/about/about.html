About KisanSetu

Empowering Indian farmers by connecting them directly with buyers.

How It Started

KisanSetu started with one simple idea — farmers deserve better access to markets, fair pricing, and direct connections with buyers.

Many small farmers struggle because of middlemen, price uncertainty, and limited technology access. We wanted to build a platform that gives farmers more control over selling their produce.

What is KisanSetu?

KisanSetu is a digital marketplace that helps farmers list crops, grains, vegetables, spices, and other agricultural products directly online.

Buyers can browse products, compare prices, contact farmers, and place orders easily.

What We Do

  • 🌾 Help farmers sell directly to buyers
  • 📦 Enable product listings and bookings
  • 💰 Promote fair pricing
  • 📱 Use technology to simplify agriculture trade
  • 🤝 Build trust between farmers and consumers
